Technology Development — AERO-1 Probe
Assembly of 20-person engineering team at our Lakeland Innovation Campus. Development of AERO-1 micro-prospector with multispectral imaging and AI-driven targeting — designed and built in Central Florida.

Series A Funding — $50M Target
Institutional funding round targeting space-focused VCs and strategic mining industry partners. NASA SBIR grant applications from our Lakeland base of operations.
Phase 4 · 2028
AERO-1 Scout Launch
AERO-1 rideshare launch from Cape Canaveral — just 2.5 hours from our Lakeland HQ. 12-month survey of target M-type asteroid to confirm platinum-group metal composition before MINER-1 deployment.
Phase 5 · 2030–2031
MINER-1 Extraction & First Return
Full extraction mission: zero-gravity anchoring, in-situ ore processing via electrostatic separation, and Earth-return capsule delivery of the first commercial asteroid-derived platinum-group metals to Earth.

Engineering the Impossible

🛰️
AERO-1 Probe
Compact 45kg autonomous scout spacecraft with multispectral and X-ray fluorescence imaging for precise mineral composition mapping from orbit.
MASS: 45KG · ΔV: 4.5 KM/S · DURATION: 36 MONTHS
⛏️
MINER-1 Platform
Modular extraction platform with percussive drill, regolith capture, and electrostatic + magnetic ore separator rated for continuous zero-gravity operations.
DRILL DEPTH: 2M · OUTPUT: 100KG/DAY · POWER: SOLAR
🧠
AI Target Engine
Proprietary machine learning pipeline trained on NASA and ESA spectral databases to rank near-Earth asteroids by extraction value and accessibility score.
DATABASE: 28,000+ NEOs · ACCURACY: 94% · REALTIME
🌕
Lunar Extraction
Parallel lunar program targeting polar water ice as in-space propellant and life support supply for NASA Artemis program participants.
TARGET: SOUTH POLE · RESOURCE: H₂O + HE-3

LOCATION: LAKELAND, FL · BAND: X + KA · RANGE: 300M KM
